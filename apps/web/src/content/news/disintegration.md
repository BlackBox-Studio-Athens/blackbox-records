Afterwise released _Disintegration_ on June 9, 2026 through BlackBox Records. The Athens band's six-track instrumental debut is available digitally on Bandcamp; Black Vinyl LP and CD are label editions.

Track sequence:

1. Equilibrium
2. StructureForms
3. LeavesBecomeDust
4. TowersBreatheForUs
5. SilverFeedsSilence
6. Renascence

All music was written and performed by Afterwise: George Stamatiou on piano and synth, Stavros Apostolou and Ilias Daramouskas on guitar, Markos Kousounadis Knousen on bass, and Giannis Avraam on drums.

Jim Spanos recorded and mixed the album at BlackBox Studio in Athens. Nikos Dimitrakakos and Jim Spanos mastered it at Unreal Studio, and Joshua Takak created the artwork.
