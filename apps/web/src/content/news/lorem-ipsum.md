_Caregivers_ is the first BlackBox Records co-release, made together with Chronoboros and Nothing to Harvest Records. The album arrived on March 13, 2026 after being recorded live at Ignite Music Studio in June 2025.

Chronoboros wrote and performed the music, with lyrics by Nikos Zalimoglou. George Christoforidis recorded, mixed, and mastered the album, and Healitwithsilver created the artwork and layout.

[View the full release details and credits](../../releases/caregivers/) or [listen and buy on Chronoboros' official Bandcamp](https://chronoboros.bandcamp.com/album/caregivers).
