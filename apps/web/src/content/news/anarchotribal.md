Ouranopithecus released _Anarchotribal_ on June 6, 2026 with BlackBox Records. The ten-track album is available digitally on Bandcamp; Vinyl is the label edition.

Track sequence:

1. Montagu
2. The Custom Of The Sea
3. Prodigy
4. Jezero
5. Bad
6. Mensur
7. Vice
8. Versa
9. Vanishing Breed
10. Albert II

The album features music by Ouranopithecus and lyrics by Zon Pletsis: Mario on drums and backing vocals, Jack on bass, and Zon Pletsis on guitar and vocals. Nina Politimou and Marianna Tzaneti appear on “Bad.”

Recorded at Atavo and Buduzi Studios, _Anarchotribal_ was produced by Ouranopithecus and Marios Adamopoulos. Adamopoulos co-engineered, recorded the bass, guitar, and vocals, and mixed the album. Kostas Ragiadakos engineered the drum recording and mastered it at TheAudioCult. Voltas and Ouranopithecus made the artwork from original front and back cover photographs by Edward S. Curtis.
