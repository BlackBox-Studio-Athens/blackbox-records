# 06.1.1 Stock Reconciliation Policy

## Source Of Truth

D1 is the only authoritative stock ledger.

- `Stock` is the current physical stock total for a `Variant`.
- `OnlineStock` is the conservative public-safe quantity exposed later to checkout and may be lower than `Stock`.
- `StockChange` records known stock movement.
- `StockCount` records an authoritative recount.
- `actor_email` comes from Cloudflare Access, not from a spreadsheet or browser-submitted field.
- `recorded_at` is generated server-side when the write is recorded.

## Spreadsheet Role

Spreadsheets are temporary capture and reporting tools only.

Allowed spreadsheet use:

- event table notes when the protected stock UI is not practical
- temporary paper-to-digital capture during offline sales
- exported reporting or manual review
- planning quantities before stock exists in D1

Forbidden spreadsheet use:

- treating a spreadsheet as live stock truth
- direct D1 edits to make D1 match a spreadsheet
- bidirectional spreadsheet sync
- importing spreadsheet rows as authoritative stock writes without operator review
- using spreadsheet names, emails, or timestamps as trusted attribution

## When To Use Each Stock Action

Use `StockChange` when the operator knows the movement.

- offline sale: negative delta
- restock or returned stock: positive delta
- damaged, lost, comped, or removed stock: negative delta
- simple correction where the delta is known

Use `StockCount` when the operator is reconciling reality.

- after an event table closes
- after stock was captured on paper or spreadsheet
- when the physical count disagrees with expected D1 `Stock`
- before checkout relies on a `Variant` after uncertain offline movement

## OnlineStock Policy

`OnlineStock` must stay conservative.

- `OnlineStock` must never exceed physical `Stock`.
- After offline sales, reduce `OnlineStock` before or during reconciliation if the team is not sure how much stock remains.
- If counts are uncertain, set `OnlineStock` lower than physical `Stock` until the recount is complete.
- Before enabling a variant for public checkout, verify `OnlineStock` through the protected stock UI.

## Operator Workflow

1. Capture offline movement on paper or spreadsheet if needed.
2. Open the protected `/stock/` UI.
3. Search the `Variant`.
4. Record known movements as `StockChange`.
5. Record recounts as `StockCount`.
6. Confirm `Stock`, `OnlineStock`, actor email, and history before checkout depends on the variant.

Staff must not edit D1 directly for normal stock operations.

## Phase Boundary

This policy does not add spreadsheet import/export, sync UI, checkout stock decrement, or order reconciliation behavior. Phase 7 may consume the conservative `OnlineStock` contract, and Phase 8 owns paid-order stock decrement and webhook reconciliation.
