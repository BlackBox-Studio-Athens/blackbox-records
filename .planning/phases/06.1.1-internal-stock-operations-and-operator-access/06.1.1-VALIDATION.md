# Phase 06.1.1 Validation

## Required Checks

- internal stock hostname is separate from the public storefront hostname
- Cloudflare Access + Google is explicit for staff-only stock access
- the Access-authenticated email header contract is explicit before stock-write routes exist
- internal write APIs are separated from public shopper APIs
- `StockChange` and `StockCount` record `actor_email` and `recorded_at`
- D1 is explicitly the only stock source of truth
- spreadsheet usage is explicitly non-authoritative
- `OnlineStock` is explicitly separated from total `Stock`

## Review Questions

- Could a shopper or unauthenticated browser hit a stock-write endpoint?
- Is Decap auth being incorrectly reused for runtime stock writes?
- Would staff still need direct DB access or spreadsheets to keep stock honest?
- Is the UI thin and operational, or drifting into a much bigger OMS?
