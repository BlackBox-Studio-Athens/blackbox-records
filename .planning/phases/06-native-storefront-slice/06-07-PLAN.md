# 06-07 Plan - Reuse Editorial Assets And Summaries Without Duplication

## Objective

Ensure the storefront reuses editorial content instead of copying it into separate commerce storage.

## Tasks

1. Reuse release assets and summaries for release-derived catalog items.
2. Reuse distro assets and summaries for distro-derived catalog items.
3. Avoid duplicate editorial storage in backend or temp storefront layers.

## Verification

- release-derived items reuse editorial content
- distro-derived items reuse editorial content
- no duplicate editorial storage is introduced
